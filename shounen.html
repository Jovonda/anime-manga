My Top Shounen Anime/Manga

Number 1

YuYuHakusho

  • Information/Recommendation
    • This anime might feel like a mystery, but it is a shounen overall. After sacrficing himself to save some else Yusuke is given a chance to regain his life, he just has to complete a few task first. Just thwart evil spirts from earth, not anything to hard.
  • Facts
    • The full name is this anime is called : YuYuHakusho: Ghost files/ Spirit Detective. While the title does have mystery in it. I can assure you there is a lot more fighting than mystery solving.
